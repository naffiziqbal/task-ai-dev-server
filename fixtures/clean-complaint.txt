COMPLAINT FOR BREACH OF CONTRACT

Plaintiff Zane & Co. Industries, Inc. ("Zane"), by and through its undersigned
counsel, brings this action against Defendants Hardman Holdings, LLC
("Hardman") and Jonathan Sidwell ("Sidwell"), and alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff Zane is a corporation organized and existing under the laws of
   the State of Delaware, with its principal place of business in New York,
   New York.

2. Defendant Hardman is a limited liability company organized and existing
   under the laws of the State of New York.

3. Defendant Sidwell is an individual residing in New York County, New York,
   and is the managing member of Hardman.

FACTUAL BACKGROUND

4. On January 14, 2024, Zane and Hardman executed a Master Services Agreement
   (the "Agreement") under which Hardman would deliver bespoke financial
   advisory services to Zane in exchange for monthly retainer payments of
   $75,000.

5. Beginning on April 1, 2024, Hardman ceased providing the services
   contemplated by the Agreement, while continuing to invoice Zane.

6. On June 12, 2024, Zane sent a written notice of default to Hardman
   pursuant to Section 9.2 of the Agreement. Hardman did not respond.

7. On August 3, 2024, Sidwell, acting on behalf of Hardman, sent an email
   to Zane's CFO stating that "the engagement is concluded, effective
   immediately," but refused to refund prepaid amounts totaling $225,000.

CLAIM FOR RELIEF — BREACH OF CONTRACT

8. The allegations of paragraphs 1 through 7 are incorporated by reference.

9. The Agreement constitutes a valid and binding contract between Zane and
   Hardman.

10. Hardman materially breached the Agreement by ceasing performance and
    refusing to refund prepaid retainer amounts.

11. Zane has been damaged in an amount not less than $225,000.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Zane respectfully requests that this Court enter
judgment in its favor and against Defendants for compensatory damages,
costs, and such other relief as the Court deems just and proper.
